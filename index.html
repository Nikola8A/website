Real Madrid

Real Madrid CF is one of the most successful and popular football clubs in the world, based in Madrid, Spain. Founded in 1902, the club has won numerous domestic and international titles, including a record number of UEFA Champions League trophies. Known for its rich history, global fanbase, and legendary players like Cristiano Ronaldo, Alfredo Di Stefano, and Zinedine Zidane, Real Madrid has built a reputation for excellence and competitiveness. The club’s home is the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, and its motto, "Hala Madrid," reflects the unwavering support and pride of its fans.

FC Liverpool

Liverpool FC is one of England's most successful and historic football clubs, founded in 1892. Based in Liverpool, the club has won numerous domestic and international titles, including multiple Premier League and UEFA Champions League trophies. Known for its passionate fanbase, symbolized by the anthem "You'll Never Walk Alone," Liverpool has a rich legacy of legendary players like Steven Gerrard, Kenny Dalglish, and Mohamed Salah. The team plays at Anfield, a stadium famous for its electrifying atmosphere and rich footballing traditions.
